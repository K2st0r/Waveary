# Waveary Brand Assets Notice

Unless a file says otherwise, official Waveary brand assets are not open-source.

## Covered Assets

This notice covers official brand and identity materials in this repository, including:

- logos and logo lockups
- portrait-card artwork
- illustrations
- iconography created as official Waveary identity
- screenshots used as official promotional materials
- other files clearly presented as official Waveary branding

Examples may include files under directories such as:

- `docs/assets/`
- `waveary-web/public/brand/`
- `waveary-web/public/images/portraits/`

## Rights Status

These assets are provided for:

- viewing the repository
- evaluating the project
- running the software locally
- preserving attribution to the original project

They are not granted for:

- rebranding another product as Waveary
- commercial redistribution under the official identity
- creating confusingly similar branded products
- reusing official portraits, logos, or promotional visuals as your own storefront or application identity
