# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Waveary Web serves two closely related user groups.

- Developers and technical teams evaluating Waveary as an open source digital life companion framework.
- Builders configuring a working local Waveary runtime so they can connect a provider, select a model, manage sessions, and test continuity behavior in the browser.

Users arrive in two modes:

- Homepage mode: they are understanding what Waveary is, why it exists, and how it differs from generic chatbot or character-wrapper products.
- Console mode: they are completing concrete tasks such as provider setup, model discovery, session management, persistence switching, import/export, and live conversation testing.

## Product Purpose

Waveary Web is the official web surface for Project Waveary.

Its purpose is to do two jobs clearly:

- Present Waveary as a serious open source digital life companion framework, not as an AI girlfriend, AI boyfriend, or generic companion app.
- Provide a practical browser-based reference shell for connecting providers, selecting models, managing persistent sessions, and interacting with the live runtime.

Success means a user can understand the framework positioning quickly on the homepage, then move into a clean operational console and a focused conversation surface without losing trust, context, or continuity.

## Brand Personality

Waveary should feel:

- continuous
- humane
- deliberate

The tone should be calm, intelligent, and grounded. It should communicate long-term memory, relationship growth, and life continuity rather than novelty, flirtation, or entertainment gimmicks. The experience should feel like a serious open source product with emotional depth, not like a themed chatbot landing page.

## Anti-references

This product should not look like:

- a generic AI SaaS template with purple gradients, glossy glow cards, and interchangeable feature marketing
- a roleplay, anime, or "AI girlfriend" landing page driven by fantasy framing
- a debug dashboard where diagnostics overpower the product story
- an overly editorial concept site where style competes with usability in the console

Avoid:

- framing Waveary as a character wrapper
- oversized decorative hero copy that crushes the page hierarchy
- repeating the same card pattern across every page and section
- mixing homepage storytelling with console management in the same first-screen experience

## Design Principles

- Separate project introduction from product operation. The homepage explains the framework, while the console manages it and the chat page uses it.
- Keep continuity visible. Memory, relationship, timeline, and persistence should feel like first-class product ideas, not hidden implementation details.
- Preserve operational trust. Forms, controls, session management, and runtime feedback must stay clear, dense, and predictable.
- Let companionship feel focused. The conversation page should privilege the active dialogue over configuration and diagnostics.
- Reinforce the repositioning everywhere. Every page should strengthen the identity of Waveary as a digital life companion framework.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Waveary Web should target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard-accessible interaction by default.

Specific expectations:

- readable contrast on warm off-white surfaces
- visible focus states for navigation, controls, and forms
- responsive layouts that remain usable on smaller screens
- reduced-motion-safe interactions
- bilingual Chinese and English presentation without resetting local runtime state
